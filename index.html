Admission Requirements

Join our learning community and take the next step in your professional journey. Review our admission requirements to ensure you're ready to start your program.

Minimum educational requirements for program admission:

  • High school diploma or equivalent (GED)
  • Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
  • English proficiency test scores (if applicable)

Required documents for your application:

  • Completed online application form
  • Personal statement or essay (500-750 words)
  • Resume or CV highlighting relevant experience
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Government-issued photo ID copy

Technical specifications for online learning:

  • Reliable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
  • Computer or laptop with webcam and microphone
  • Updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Basic computer literacy and navigation skills
  • Access to required software (provided during enrollment)

Course-specific prerequisites and recommended experience:

  • Basic understanding of computers and internet usage
  • Willingness to learn and adapt to new technologies
  • Time commitment of 10-15 hours per week
  • Previous work experience preferred but not required
  • Completion of placement assessment (if applicable)

Financial obligations and payment options:

  • Tuition payment or approved payment plan
  • Registration fee (non-refundable)
  • Financial aid documentation (if applicable)
  • Scholarship application materials
  • Employer sponsorship forms (if applicable)

Important dates and timeline for applications:

  • Early admission deadline: December 1st
  • Regular admission deadline: February 15th
  • Late admission deadline: April 30th (if space available)
  • International student deadline: January 15th
  • Financial aid priority deadline: March 1st
